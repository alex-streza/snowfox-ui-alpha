# Button

Everyone knows what a buttons does, it allow to take actions in the app, often used for navigation, forms, or any other task.
In SnowFox we have 3 base variants `primary`, `outline` and `text`, but also other 4 for intents `info`, `success`, `warning` and `error`.

You can customize the variants by adding a custom tailwind.config.js, more info in the [config page](/docs/start--sfge)
